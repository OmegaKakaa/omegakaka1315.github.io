Welcome!

Love Community Montessori School is a creche school for children between the age of 3months to 6years. They are taught how read and write to better their grammar and vocabularies. There are other activities which help your kidz to build their mental state such as mental arithmetic's, spelling bee, reading competition, reading club and dancing to build their physical state. All these makes school activities very interesting.

Our Mission

To enhance and assist the child equip or develop his or her God given potentials, by training him/her to have the requisite knowledge of academic execellence and also help them cultivatethe habit of reading and write.

Our Vision

To be among the leading quality schools in the eastern region at large.
